Our Story

Made with flour, butter,
& a whole lot of love.

MUI Bakery was born in the heart of Bhaktapur in 2014, when our founder Sunita Shrestha began baking traditional Nepali sweets alongside French-inspired pastries in her family kitchen. What started as a labor of love grew into Bhaktapur's most beloved neighbourhood bakery.

Every item we bake is handmade from scratch, using locally sourced wheat, farm-fresh eggs, and seasonal fruits from the Kathmandu Valley. We believe great baking is an act of care — and we pour that care into everything we make.
